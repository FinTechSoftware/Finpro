# QR Code

QR codes are a convenient way to share brief pieces of information with users who can easily scan them with a smartphone. With most smartphones equipped with built-in QR code readers, users can simply point their camera at a QR code to decode it and access a website, dial a phone number, read a message, or perform other actions. To generate a QR code and display it, you can use the Canvas API.
